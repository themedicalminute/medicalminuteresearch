Who We Are

About Us

The Medical Minute is a student-led organization and research-based platform dedicated to making accurate, evidence-based medical knowledge more accessible, engaging, and easy to understand for students—by students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate and inspire the next generation of healthcare leaders by sharing trustworthy medical information through research-based content, myth-vs.-fact education, health awareness campaigns, community outreach, and educational workshops.

Our Impact

Beyond education, we are committed to making a meaningful impact by supporting communities through fundraising, advocacy initiatives, volunteer service, and partnerships that promote health awareness and improve access to reliable medical information.

Trustworthy medical knowledge —
for students, by students.
